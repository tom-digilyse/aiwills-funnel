1b. Measure brand on the live site (most accurate)

Fonts and background colours can only be read reliably from the rendered page, not the page source. Two ways to measure, run either on the client's site then paste the result below: (A) the one-click bookmark, or (B) the console snippet, which always works even on sites that block bookmarks.

A. One-click bookmark. Drag this to your bookmarks bar once, then click it on the client's site: Measure AI Wills brand ← drag me up. If nothing happens on a site, use method B.

B. Console snippet (most reliable). On the client's site press F12, open the Console tab, paste this and press Enter. If Chrome warns about pasting, type allow pasting first. The result copies to your clipboard automatically.
